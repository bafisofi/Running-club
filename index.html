Who are we?

We are a group of people who love to run! Join our running club, where all levels of experience are celebrated. Whether you're a former athlete or a recreational jogger, our club running team is the perfect fit for you. Join us and embark on exhilarating journeys together!

Why to join us?

Practice and Races

Join us for voluntary practice sessions Mon-Fri at 6:30 PM at Serenity Gardens Park. We send out weekly emails with the practice schedule. During weekends, we participate in a diverse range of races throughout the year. These events provide us with opportunities to compete against other running clubs, fostering a spirit of friendly competition and camaraderie.

What Sets Us Apart

In addition to training and racing, we organize various social events throughout the year. Previous events have included dinners, the Sweet Mile, baking contests, ice skating, camps and picnics. Enjoy the freedom to choose your own path: There are no coaches, no obligatory attendance, no forced competitions, and no prescribed workouts. Participate in races on your terms.
